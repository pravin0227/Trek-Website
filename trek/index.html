What's Your Trip?

Why trek with us?

With a diverse array of landscapes, terrains, culture and people; a trekking tour in India is one of the most eye-opening and magical experiences you can ever have on this planet. Hiding in its store, some of the most alluring and exhilarating treks and with each trek offering something distinct and unfolding a new mystery, there is no trekking destination that can match up to the magic and charm of Indian treks. So, if you are also an ardent trekker and adventures, challenges and mysteries fascinate you, come and join us on one of the most electrifying trekking adventures of your lifetime.

Being active in the domain of travel for more than 30 years, we specialize in organizing Himalayan Hiking & Adventure Tours. Our team of experienced staff leaves no stones unturned in providing you world-class trekking expeditions in Himalayan regions of Ladakh, Sikkim, Garhwal, Kumaon and Himachal. With our deals and packages, you can be sure of getting top-notch services combined with unmatched sightseeing tours, top class accommodations and exclusive arrangements for licensed tour guides, porters and trekking & camping equipment. Well! That is not all, we also help you obtain necessary permissions and permits from concerned authorities wherever required. So, if you have that zeal and courage to takeover the heights of lofty mountain peaks, get ready to have the most enriching and thrilling trekking experiences with us.
