Q. Is one day enough for Matheran?

A. Well, there are no vehicles in Matheran and it is
quite impossible to explore it in a single day. You can
spend as much time as you want in this place in
Maharashtra, from one day to five

Q. What is special about Matheran?

A. Matheran is one of the top hill stations
in Maharashtra where people from Mumbai
and Pune head to for a weekend getaway.
The untouched beauty of Matheran
attracts many tourists and hikers
from all around India

Q.Is Rajmachi Trek safe?

Precaution to be taken at Rajmachi:
Do not go for this trek alone. There have
been few cases of armed robbery on the way
to Rajmachi from Lonavala, as the road
travels through jungle and is often empty.
It will be safe to travel in a group of at
least 3 and more

Q. How can I go to Rajmachi?
Lonavala is nearest railway station to reach
Rajmachi fort. Once you are at
Lonavala station you can hire a taxi or private
cab to reach Rajmachi fort

Q. How many steps are there in Pratapgad?
To get to this fort, you need to climb like
450-500 steps. And mind you, these are steps
with decent height. So a strict no for older folks.
There is this 'Bhavani mata' temple on 200 steps which
is worth going
